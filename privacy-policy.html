Privacy Policy – 2048+

Effective date: 2026-02-26

2048+ ("the App") is a free puzzle game developed by Trong Pong. This page describes how the App handles information.

Information We Collect

The App itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. The only data stored locally on your device is your high score, saved in the app's private storage and never shared with anyone.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays advertisements served by Google AdMob (Google LLC). AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

  • Advertising ID (GAID / IDFA)
  • Device information (model, OS version, language)
  • IP address and general location (country/region)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

This data is collected and processed by Google under their own privacy policy. You can review Google's practices and opt out of personalized advertising at any time:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Opt out of personalized ads

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. AdMob is configured to comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the Google Play Families Policy where applicable.

Third-Party Links

The App does not contain links to third-party websites or services beyond the advertisements served by AdMob.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by an updated effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue at our GitHub repository.
