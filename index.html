Privacy Policy

The Expedition — Last updated: March 15, 2026

This privacy policy describes how The Expedition ("the Game", "we", "us") handles information when you use our mobile and desktop game.

1. Information We Collect

We aim to collect as little data as possible. The Game may collect:

  • Analytics data — Anonymous gameplay statistics (e.g., levels completed, play session length) to help us improve game balance and performance. No personally identifiable information is included.
  • Device information — Basic device type, OS version, and screen resolution for compatibility and bug fixing purposes.
  • Crash reports — Anonymous crash logs to help us identify and fix bugs.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any personal contact information.
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not require account creation or login.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.

3. In-App Purchases

The Game may offer in-app purchases through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple or Google. We do not receive, store, or have access to your payment information.

4. Advertising

The Game may display ads provided by third-party ad networks. These networks may use anonymous identifiers to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings.

5. Children's Privacy

The Game is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

6. Data Storage and Security

All game save data is stored locally on your device. Analytics data, if collected, is transmitted securely and stored in aggregate form without personally identifiable information.

7. Third-Party Services

The Game may use the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store — for distribution and payments
  • Ad networks — for displaying advertisements

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing the data they collect.

8. Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete all local game data by uninstalling the Game.
  • Opt out of personalized ads via your device's privacy settings.
  • Contact us with any privacy-related questions or requests.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
